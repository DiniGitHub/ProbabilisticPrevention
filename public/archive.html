Probabilistic Prevention

Predicting and Preventing Health Risks!

I want to build a web application that assesses health risks based on demographical and habit information the user enters. This project will mostly make use of conditional probability in determining which diseases users are at top risk for, and then suggest going to doctor check-ups/screenings. For example, if you were a woman above the age of 30, my application would likely suggest you should get checked for breast cancer etc. I believe this will be socially beneficial because I want it to encourage people to live healthier lives and go to check-ups more frequently. I DO NOT want to cause panic or diagnose anyone with a disease based on the input information but simply inform/remind them of the risks that come with certain choices (smoking, drinking, etc.) and encourage healthy behaviour.
